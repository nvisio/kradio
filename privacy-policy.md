# Privacy Policy

*Effective Date: April 10, 2026*

K-Radio Tuner ("the App") is developed by HongKee Moon. This Privacy Policy describes how the App handles your information.

## Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. There are no user accounts, analytics, or tracking of any kind.

## Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device using UserDefaults:

- Your last played station (to resume playback)
- Custom stations you add (name and stream URL)
- Station customizations (hidden or edited stations)
- Subscription status (cached for offline access)

This data never leaves your device except via Apple's Watch Connectivity framework to sync settings to your paired Apple Watch.

## Network Connections

The App connects to the following services to stream radio:

- Korean radio streams via radio.bsod.kr (KBS, MBC, SBS, CBS, TBS, EBS, YTN)
- BBC radio streams via Akamai CDN (as-hls-ww-live.akamaized.net)
- Any custom stream URLs you add manually

These connections are for audio playback only. No personal data is transmitted.

## In-App Purchases

The App offers auto-renewable subscriptions for Apple Watch functionality. Subscriptions are managed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We do not have access to your payment information.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
